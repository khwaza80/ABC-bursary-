About ABC Bursary

Empowering Futures, Building the Nation

Who We Are

The ABC Bursary Programme is a transformative initiative led by the Gauteng provincial government, aimed at empowering talented South African youth through equitable access to education. Our focus is on those with exceptional potential, integrity, and the drive to uplift communities. The ABC bursary is responsible for building a pool of skilled, capable and ethical workforce for the gauteng City Region. This is done through different interventions, i.e., bursaries, internships, learnerships, skills programmes, etc. The ABC bursary programme is open to all qualifying youth, with particular preference given to youth from Gauteng, who wish to study for an undergraduate and/or post-graduate programme at an accredited and registered Post School Education and Training institution in South Africa. The bursary covers full cost of study, with conditions, as stipulated in the bursary contract

Our Mission

To remove financial barriers and ensure access to tertiary education for academically gifted and financially disadvantaged students. We seek to cultivate the next generation of ethical, innovative, and skilled professionals.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every youth has a fair chance to thrive through education, regardless of economic background — building a capable, diverse, and inclusive knowledge economy.

What We Offer

The ABC Bursary covers:

  • 📚 Full tuition and academic fees
  • 🏠 University-approved accommodation
  • 🍱 Meals and textbooks
  • 💸 Monthly living allowance (stipend)
  • 🧭 Mentorship and career coaching
  • 💼 Internship opportunities and workplace exposure
  • 🛟 Rescue Pool for emergency or alternative funding

Our Impact

Since our launch, we have:

  • 🎓 Supported over 15,000 students
  • 🌍 Created one of the largest alumni networks of public bursary recipients in SA
  • 🤝 Fostered partnerships with public and private institutions
  • 💡 Sparked entrepreneurial ventures and social upliftment projects through alumni
